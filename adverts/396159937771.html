Eric Sole's Cabildo wall light for Artemide was awarded the Good Design Award at the Chicago Atheneum in 2008. The lights sculptural, curving body is constructed from white painted die-cast aluminium to beautifully complement modern interiors. The integrated LED diffuses an indirect halo of light, creating a play of light on the wall around the lamp. Minimalist yet at the same time dramatic, the Cabildo wall light is perfect for contemporary bedrooms, hallways, living rooms and offices. Available in two soft white LED temperatures ideal for a warm and cosy atmosphere.
